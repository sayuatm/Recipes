Mango Pina Colada Smoothie

Description

This smoothie is my take on an alcohol-free pina colada.

Ingredients

  • 1 mango - peeled, seeded, and cubed
  • 1 (14 ounce) can coconut milk
  • 1.25 cups ice cubes
  • 1.25 cups pineapple juice
  • 0.5 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
